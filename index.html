TIME MACHINE

A glance through the site

Time Machine is a tribute to the past: dive to find alternative pieces of history from the place you are located. Try it and investigate within your area, news from where the wind has taken your location and what has been re-built by our civilization now. Click to 'Dive in to the past' and look forward to delving back through time in as you explore your location. Remember say yes to your location , so you are not stuck in the present!
